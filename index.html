LOONA, also known as Girl of the Month is a 12 member Korean girl group under the company BlackBerry Creative (BBC). The members of the group are Heejin, Hyunjin, Haseul, Yeojin, ViVi, Kim Lip, Jinsoul, Choerry, Yves, Chuu, Gowon, and Olivia Hye. One member was revealed once a month including a solo song, and an album along with it hence the name “Girl of the Month”. They officially debuted as a group on their concert LOONAbirth with the release of the EP [+ +] and its title tracked entitled “Hi High” on August 20, 2018. Since then, they have made more albums and EPs called [X X], [#], [12:00], and [&]. They have also made 3 official sub-units called LOONA ⅓, ODD EYE CIRCLE, and yyxy.
