Loona 1/3 is the first sub-unit girl group from loona released by BlackBerryCreative. This is a 4 member sub-unit consisting of Loona members
Heejin, Hyunjin, Haseul, and Vivi. They debuted on March 12, 2017 on Inkigayo with their song Love & Live. On April 27, 2017, Loona 1/3
had a comeback which is a repackaged version of their first album, titled Love & Evil. Yeojin was revealed to be the / in 1/3,
although she is not a member of the sub-unit itself.
