LOOΠΔ ODD EYE CIRCLE


Loona ODD EYE CIRCLE is the second sub-unit from the girl group Loona. They debuted with members Kim Lip, Jinsoul, and Choerry.
They debuted on September 21, 2017 with their mini-album Mix&Match. They released another repackaged
album on October 31, 2017 entitled Max&Match.
