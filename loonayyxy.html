Loona yyxy (wai-wai-bai-wai) is the third and final pre-debut sub-unit of Loona released by BlackBerryCreative.
This was a 4 member sub-unit consisting of Yves, Chuu, Gowon, and Olivia Hye. They debuted
on May 30, 2018 with the mini-album beauty&thebeat.
